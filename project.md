## Left Fence Constraint

The left-side fence limits how high the lawn can be raised. Before levelling:

1. Check left fence post depth (dig/probe at base)
2. If post depth < fill required: either accept a level step at left lawn boundary, or replace posts before raising ground level
3. Option: install corten steel or sleeper retaining edge at patio/lawn junction

---

## Electrical Notes (IMPORTANT — Part P)

- Any new circuit added to a consumer unit requires Part P Building Regulations notification
- Contact local building control BEFORE starting electrical work
- DIY electrical work to a new circuit: either use a Part P registered electrician, OR self-certify (if competent person) via local authority building control
- Outbuilding fusebox: check it has spare capacity and RCD protection
- **Spec:** 2.5mm² 3-core SWA armoured cable, 20mm steel or MDPE conduit, IP65 fittings throughout
- **Burial depth:** 450mm minimum in soft ground, any depth under paving subbase is acceptable

## Materials Specification

### Natural Stone Paving

- **Area:** ~50 sqm + 10% wastage = ~55 sqm to order
- **Spec:** Tumbled granite setts OR Kandla Grey sandstone, 50mm thick, F2 frost-rated, R11+ slip
- **Laying spec:** 150mm MOT Type 1 subbase (compacted), 50mm sharp sand/cement mortar bed (1:5), full contact (no voids), flexible exterior pointing mortar for joints
- **Note:** Order all from one batch — natural stone varies between batches

### Pergola

- **Size:** 4×3m or 4×4m (confirm after measuring right-side zone)
- **Spec:** Powder-coated aluminium, anthracite, motorised louvres, integrated drainage gutters, base plates (not ground spikes)
- **Frame:** 100×100mm posts, 6063 aluminium, 60+ micron powder coat
- **Footings:** 4× C20 concrete pads, 450×450×700mm deep, post socket cast in. Pour BEFORE paving.

### Raised Planters

- 4× large: 1500–2000mm × 600–800mm × 400mm min depth
- **Material:** Corten steel (recommended) or hardwood oak
- Must have drainage holes + landscape fabric liner

### Lawn Edging

- EverEdge 3mm galvanised steel, 150mm deep
- ~30 linear metres (measure final curve before ordering)
- Set 5–10mm below finished turf level

### Turf

- **Area:** ~70–80 sqm (central + left areas)
- Order after fine levelling is complete (late June at earliest)
- SE England June–July: fine for turfing with daily watering

### Climbing Plants (NWW facing = SE/S-SE opening — excellent sunny aspect)

- **Star Jasmine (Trachelospermum jasminoides) × 1–2** — strongly recommended for SE-facing. Evergreen, intensely fragrant, white star flowers June–Aug. Perfect for pergola.
- Clematis montana 'Alba' × 1 — fast coverage while jasmine establishes
- Hydrangea petiolaris × 1 — optional for back fence if shaded

### Outdoor Furniture

- **Spec:** Large corner sofa, PE rattan/aluminium, charcoal, Olefin/Sunbrella cushions, quick-dry foam, fitted winter cover
- Check footprint vs patio dimensions before ordering

### Children's Swing Set

- EN71 certified, hot-dip galvanised + powder coat, blue steel
- Rubber safety tiles beneath (RoSPA requirement on hard ground)
